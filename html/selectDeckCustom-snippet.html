Card Selection

Some cards are not good when playing aginst the Horde, either because they just don't do anything, or because they're too good. If you have these cards or cards like them in your deck, I suggest replacing them with something else before you upload. You can of course have your own custom rules to make them more or less effective if you prefer.

  • Smothering Tithe, Rhystic Study, Mystic Remora ...
  • Propaganda/Ghostly Prison/Windborn Muse ...
  • Stormtide Leviathan, Magus of the Moat
  • Crawlspace, Ensnaring Bridge
  • Mindcrank

When pasting your deck, format your deck listing so that it has one unique card per line and looks like one of the following
